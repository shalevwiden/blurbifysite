Wether its ideas, thoughts, notes, quotes, or insights, Blurbify turns text into a Blurb you can customize and animate.
You can also add an image too so not just text.
Blurbify was created from the simple idea that sharing text content is easy and fun, and the current text content platforms aren't as universally used as Instagram.

Customize Blurbs

Set the background and border to any color, and adjust the text size to ensure the Blurb is the right height.

Customizable Flying Image

Toggle a flying image that will fly across the screen. Change (or keep) the default Blurbify plane, and specify speed, position, and size of the flying image as it flies over or under the Blurb on the screen.

While you could use truly a massive variety of ways to share text, Blurbify is a customizable, fast, and recognizable format.

There are many ways Blurbs are recognizable across the content ocean that is Social Media:

  • Profile Picture Gradients:
    Each Blurb has a profile picture gradient. For Blurbs that belong to confirmed users, there is a distinct blue, purple, pink, gradient.
    For Blurbs from Blurb Templates and Blurbify Studio there is a red pinkish gradient.
  • Styling:
    Rounded corners, a variety of colors, and the icon layout.
  • Displaying the date in an unconventional way:
    On Blurbs, you can see the date the Blurb was made, as its expressed through the viewcount number in the icons bar.
    Why? Its fun to switch things up sometimes.
    And yeah people might be confused at first but they'll eventually catch on.
