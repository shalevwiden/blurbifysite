What are Blurb templates? Blurb templates come with preassigned, non changable usernames, names, and profile pictures, but with updateable content to create a variety of blurbs. Each Blurb template comes with default color styling which can be changed.

In addition, each Blurb template comes with a unique default flyingimage. The flying image can also be changed.
